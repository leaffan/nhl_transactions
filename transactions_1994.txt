Toronto Maple Leafs: Acquired center Mats Sundin, defenseman Garth Butcher, left wing Todd Warriner and a first-round pick (previously acquired from Philadelphia, later traded to Washington) in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft from the Quebec Nordiques for left wing Wendel Clark, defenseman Sylvain Lefebvre, right wing Landon Wilson and a first-round pick (22nd overall, Nordiques selected Jeffrey Kealty) in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft.
