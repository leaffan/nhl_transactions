
Dec 12, 1998
Philadelphia Flyers: Acquired center Daymond Langkow and right wing Mikael Renberg from the Tampa Bay Lightning for centers Mike Sillinger and Chris Gratton.

Jul 29, 1998
Washington Capitals: Signed defenseman Dmitri Mironov, who had been with the Detroit Red Wings.

Mar 24, 1998
Colorado Avalanche: Acquired right wing Tom Fitzgerald from the Florida Panthers for right wing Mark Parrish and a third-round pick (63rd overall, Panthers selected Lance Ward) in the 1998 NHL Entry Draft.
Detroit Red Wings: Acquired defenseman Dmitri Mironov from the Anaheim Mighty Ducks for defenseman Jamie Pushor and a fourth-round pick (112th overall, Mighty Ducks selected Viktor Wallin) in the 1998 NHL Entry Draft.
