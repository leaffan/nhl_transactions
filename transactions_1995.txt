
Jan 18, 1995
Ottawa Senators: Acquired goaltender Don Beaupre from the Washington Capitals for a fifth-round pick (105th overall, Capitals selected Benoit Gratton) in the 1995 NHL Entry Draft.

Apr 7, 1995
Ottawa Senators: Acquired center Martin Straka from the Pittsburgh Penguins for defenseman Norm Maciver and center Troy Murray.

Jul 8, 1995
Toronto Maple Leafs: Acquired defenseman Larry Murphy from the Pittsburgh Penguins for defenseman Dmitri Mironov and a second-round pick (later traded to New Jersey) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.
