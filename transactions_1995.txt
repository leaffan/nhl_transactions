
Dec 6, 1995
Colorado Avalanche: Acquired goaltender Patrick Roy and right wing Mike Keane from the Montreal Canadiens for goaltender Jocelyn Thibault, right wing Andrei Kovalenko and left wing Martin Rucinsky.

Nov 16, 1995
Philadelphia Flyers: Acquired right wing Pat Falloon from the San Jose Sharks for left wing Martin Spanhel and first- (later traded to Winnipeg via Buffalo; 24th overall) and fourth-round (later traded to Buffalo; 104th overall) picks in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.
San Jose Sharks: Acquired defenseman Doug Bodger from the Buffalo Sabres for left wings Vaclav Varada and Martin Spanhel and first- (previously acquired from Philadelphia, later traded to Winnipeg; 24th overall) and fourth-round (previously acquired from Philadelphia; 104th overall, Sabres selected Mike Martone) picks in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.

Aug 30, 1995
Toronto Maple Leafs: Acquired defenseman Dmitry Yushkevich and a second-round pick (50th overall, Maple Leafs selected Francis Larivee) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft from the Philadelphia Flyers for first- (15th overall, Flyers selected Dainius Zubrus) and fourth-round (previously acquired from Los Angeles, later traded to Los Angeles; 82nd overall) picks in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft and a second-round pick (30th overall, Flyers selected Jean-Marc Pelletier) in the 1997 NHL Entry Draft.

Aug 4, 1995
Edmonton Oilers: Acquired goaltender Curtis Joseph and right wing Mike Grier from the St. Louis Blues for a first-round pick (previously acquired from St. Louis; 14th overall, Blues selected Marty Reasoner) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft and a first-round pick (later traded to Los Angeles; 15th overall) in the 1997 NHL Entry Draft.

Jul 28, 1995
Edmonton Oilers: Acquired a first-round pick (later traded back to St. Louis; 14th overall) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft and a first-round pick (later traded to Los Angeles via St. Louis; 15th overall) in the 1997 NHL Entry Draft from the St. Louis Blues as compensation for the signing of left wing Shayne Corson.
St. Louis Blues: Signed left wing Shayne Corson, who had been with the Edmonton Oilers.

Jul 8, 1995
Los Angeles Kings: Acquired right wing Dmitri Khristich and goaltender Byron Dafoe from the Washington Capitals for first- (4th overall, Capitals selected Alexandre Volchkov) and fourth-round (previously acquired from Dallas; 83rd overall, Capitals selected Justin Davis) picks in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.
Toronto Maple Leafs: Acquired defenseman Larry Murphy from the Pittsburgh Penguins for defenseman Dmitri Mironov and a second-round pick (later traded to New Jersey) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.

Apr 7, 1995
Ottawa Senators: Acquired center Martin Straka from the Pittsburgh Penguins for defenseman Norm Maciver and center Troy Murray.

Apr 4, 1995
Anaheim Mighty Ducks: Acquired center Mike Sillinger and defenseman Jason York from the Detroit Red Wings for left wing Stu Grimson, defenseman Mark Ferner and a sixth-round pick (144th overall, Red Wings selected Magnus Nilsson) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.

Feb 27, 1995
Detroit Red Wings: Acquired left wing Bob Errey from the San Jose Sharks for a fifth-round pick (130th overall, Sharks selected Michal Bros) in the 1995 NHL Entry Draft.

Feb 17, 1995
Dallas Stars: Acquired left wing Mike Donnelly and a seventh-round pick (163rd overall, Stars selected Eoin McInerney) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft from the Los Angeles Kings for a fourth-round pick (later traded to Washington; 83rd overall) in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.

Jan 18, 1995
Ottawa Senators: Acquired goaltender Don Beaupre from the Washington Capitals for a fifth-round pick (105th overall, Capitals selected Benoit Gratton) in the 1995 NHL Entry Draft.
