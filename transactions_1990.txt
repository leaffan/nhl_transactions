
Mar 5, 1990
Buffalo Sabres: Acquired defenseman Jay Wells and a fourth-round pick (72nd overall, Sabres selected Peter Ambroziak) from the Philadelphia Flyers for right wing Kevin Maguire and a second-round pick (40th overall, Flyers selected Mikael Renberg) in the 1990 NHL Entry Draft.

Jun 16, 1990
Toronto Maple Leafs: Acquired right wing Kevin Maguire and an eighth-round pick (160th overall, Maple Leafs selected Dmitri Mironov) in the 1991 NHL Entry Draft from the Philadelphia Flyers for a third-round pick (52nd overall, Flyers selected Al Kinisky) in the 1990 NHL Entry Draft.
