

Mar 8, 2000
Phoenix Coyotes: Acquired right wing Mikael Renberg from the Philadelphia Flyers for right wing Rick Tocchet.

Mar 9, 2000
Buffalo Sabres: Acquired center Chris Gratton and a second-round pick (32nd overall, Sabres selected Derek Roy) in the 2001 NHL Entry Draft from the Tampa Bay Lightning for centers Brian Holzinger and Wayne Primeau, defenseman Cory Sarich and right wing Alexander Kharitonov.

Jun 24, 2000
Tampa Bay Lightning: Acquired goaltender Kevin Weekes, defenseman Kristian Kudroc and a second-round pick (later traded to Phoenix) in the 2001 NHL Entry Draft from the New York Islanders for a first-, fourth- and seventh-round picks (5th, 105th and 202nd overall, Islanders selected Raffi Torres, Vladimir Gorbunov and Ryan Caldwell) in the 2000 NHL Entry Draft.
